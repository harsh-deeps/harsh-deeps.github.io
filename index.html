About Me

Hello! My name is Harshdeep and I enjoy exploring the intersection of quantum computing and machine learning. My journey in quantum research began during my studies where I became fascinated with the potential of quantum algorithms to solve complex problems.

Today, I'm focused on developing and optimizing quantum algorithms for machine learning applications, working with technologies like Qiskit, Cirq, and PennyLane to push the boundaries of what's possible in quantum computation.

I'm passionate about contributing to the quantum open-source community and sharing knowledge through research papers and technical blogs.
